GDAP relationships don’t scale themselves. What works fine for five customer tenants becomes an operational bottleneck at fifty, and a genuine risk at two hundred when expiring relationships start slipping through the cracks. Microsoft’s Partner Center handles GDAP setup well enough for individual relationships, but it wasn’t built for MSPs managing sprawling multi-tenant environments.
