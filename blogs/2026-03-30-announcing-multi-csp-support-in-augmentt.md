<p>We’ve just released a new capability in Augmentt: multi-CSP support.</p>



<p>This update is designed to help MSPs manage GDAP across multiple Microsoft environments in a way that actually reflects how they operate today.</p>



<h2 class="wp-block-heading">Why We Built This</h2>



<p>Over time, the structure of MSP environments can change, whether due to an acquisition, adding new tenants, or Microsoft environments are brought under new management. In many cases, those environments are not immediately consolidated. Some are intentionally kept separate for operational or business reasons.</p>



<p>The result is that many MSPs are now managing:</p>



<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li>Multiple tenants</li>



<li>Multiple CSP relationships</li>



<li>Environments that operate independently</li>
</ul>



<p>This is no longer an exception. It is a common operating model.</p>



<p>However, most tools, including those used to manage access and security, were designed with a much simpler structure in mind.</p>



<h2 class="wp-block-heading">Where GDAP Gets Complicated</h2>



<p>GDAP is an important step forward for managing access securely. It allows MSPs to apply role-based access instead of relying on broad administrative permissions.</p>



<p>But as environments expand, managing GDAP becomes more difficult.</p>



<p>Across multiple tenants and CSP relationships, teams often run into:</p>



<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li>Inconsistent role assignments</li>



<li>Limited visibility into access across environments</li>



<li>Manual processes that do not scale</li>



<li>Increased risk from over-permissioned accounts</li>
</ul>



<p>Individually, each tenant is manageable. Collectively, they become harder to control.</p>



<h2 class="wp-block-heading">What Multi-CSP Support Enables</h2>



<p>With multi-CSP support in Augmentt, MSPs can now manage GDAP across multiple CSP environments from a single platform.</p>



<p>This allows you to:</p>



<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li>View GDAP relationships across all tenants in one place</li>



<li>Apply consistent roles and configurations across environments</li>



<li>Reduce manual work when managing access</li>



<li>Maintain better visibility and control as your environment grows</li>
</ul>



<p>Instead of treating each CSP relationship as a separate operational silo, you can manage them together with a consistent approach.</p>



<h2 class="wp-block-heading">Designed for Real-World MSP Environments</h2>



<p>This feature is not about introducing a new concept. It is about addressing a reality that already exists.</p>



<p>MSPs are operating across multiple environments, and those environments are often fragmented, so they do not always converge into a single structure.</p>



<p>Multi-CSP support is built to help you manage that complexity directly, without requiring you to simplify your environment first.</p>



<h2 class="wp-block-heading">Moving Forward</h2>



<p>As MSPs continue to grow, the ability to manage access consistently across multiple environments becomes more important.</p>



<p>GDAP remains a critical part of that. Multi-CSP support makes it practical to manage GDAP at scale.</p>



<p>If you are working across multiple tenants or CSP relationships, this update is available now in Augmentt.</p>
