<p>2025 was a milestone year for&nbsp;Augmentt.&nbsp;</p>



<p>Over the past year, we focused on helping MSPs work smarter, move faster, and stay ahead of security risks in Microsoft 365. From stronger automation to clearer security insights, everything we released was built to give MSPs better control,&nbsp;without adding more complexity.&nbsp;</p>



<p>Here’s&nbsp;a deeper look at what we shipped in 2025 and why it matters.&nbsp;</p>

<h2 class="wp-block-heading">Clearer, Stronger Security Across Microsoft 365&nbsp;</h2>



<p>Security was a top priority throughout 2025.&nbsp;MSPs need to quickly understand risk across tenants and take action without jumping between tools.&nbsp;This year,&nbsp;Augmentt&nbsp;made that easier.&nbsp;</p>



<h3 class="wp-block-heading">Better Visibility and Control for Defender&nbsp;</h3>



<p>In August, we rolled out new tools that simplify how MSPs review and manage&nbsp;<a href="https://www.augmentt.com/microsoft-defender-policies/" rel="noreferrer noopener" target="_blank">Microsoft Defender policies</a>.&nbsp;</p>



<p>Instead of manually checking settings in each tenant, MSPs can now see where protections are missing or misconfigured in one place. They can also roll out consistent Defender policies across multiple customers, helping ensure every tenant meets the same security standard.&nbsp;</p>



<h3 class="wp-block-heading">Bringing Intune Insights into&nbsp;Augmentt&nbsp;</h3>



<p>In November,&nbsp;we introduced&nbsp;<a href="https://www.augmentt.com/augmentt-intune-autopilot/" rel="noreferrer noopener" target="_blank">Intune Autopilot</a>.&nbsp;</p>



<p>This update pulls key Intune data directly into&nbsp;Augmentt, giving MSPs a clearer view of device security and compliance. With everything in one dashboard,&nbsp;it’s&nbsp;easier to spot issues, understand risk, and improve device protection&nbsp;without switching between portals.&nbsp;</p>



<h3 class="wp-block-heading">Security Standards That Scale&nbsp;</h3>



<p>Throughout the year, we continued to expand security&nbsp;baselining.&nbsp;</p>



<p>MSPs can now create standard Entra ID group baselines and apply them across tenants. This helps keep important controls&nbsp;(like Conditional Access, Defender, and Intune)&nbsp;aligned and consistent, even as environments grow.&nbsp;</p>



<h2 class="wp-block-heading">Automated Responses and Alerts&nbsp;</h2>



<p>In 2025, alerting became more than just notifications.&nbsp;Augmentt&nbsp;helped MSPs&nbsp;identify&nbsp;risks earlier and respond automatically when it mattered most.&nbsp;&nbsp;</p>



<h3 class="wp-block-heading">Catching MFA Gaps Early&nbsp;</h3>



<p>In May, we launched MFA registration&nbsp;alerts.&nbsp;These help MSPs close security gaps before they lead to account compromise, reducing risk without adding manual checks.&nbsp;</p>



<h3 class="wp-block-heading">Alert Auto-Remediation&nbsp;</h3>



<p>In July, alerting took another step forward with automatic remediation.&nbsp;</p>



<p>When Microsoft 365 detects serious issues,&nbsp;like suspicious sign-ins,&nbsp;Augmentt&nbsp;can respond on its own. MSPs stay in control by setting rules for:&nbsp;</p>



<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li>Which alerts trigger action </li>



<li>What steps should be taken </li>



<li>When automation runs </li>



<li>Whether settings apply globally or per tenant </li>
</ul>



<p>This helps protect customers even outside business hours.&nbsp;</p>



<h2 class="wp-block-heading">Easier User Management with Engage&nbsp;</h2>



<p>User management also saw meaningful improvements in 2025, especially within&nbsp;<a href="https://www.augmentt.com/engage-autopilot/" rel="noreferrer noopener" target="_blank">Engage Autopilot</a>.&nbsp;</p>



<h3 class="wp-block-heading">Planned&nbsp;Offboardings, Less Stress&nbsp;</h3>



<p>MSPs can now schedule&nbsp;offboardings&nbsp;ahead of time, making it easier to handle employee exits smoothly and securely,&nbsp;without last-minute rushes.&nbsp;</p>



<h3 class="wp-block-heading">Faster Onboarding with Reusable Templates&nbsp;</h3>



<p>Onboarding workflows became quicker and more consistent with templates. MSPs can set up users the same way every time, saving effort and reducing errors.&nbsp;</p>



<p>More day-to-day user actions were also added directly into&nbsp;Augmentt, cutting down on tool switching.&nbsp;</p>



<h3 class="wp-block-heading">Secure Access When Users Need It&nbsp;</h3>



<p>Temporary Access Passes quickly became one of the most popular features of the year.&nbsp;</p>



<p>They allow MSPs to grant short-term, secure access without weakening security;&nbsp;perfect for onboarding, support, or recovery scenarios.&nbsp;</p>
